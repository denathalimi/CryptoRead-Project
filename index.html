Frequently Asked question

What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency. Cryptocurrency is a digital payment system that doesn't rely on banks to verify transactions.

Is cryptocurrency safe?

Cryptocurrencies are usually built using blockchain technology. The underlying blockchain technology is inherently secure.

How does cryptocurrency work?

Cryptocurrencies run on a distributed public ledger called blockchain, a record of all transactions updated by currency holders.

What can you buy with cryptocurrency?

With Bitcoin it's possible to buy everything from a cup of coffee to a computer or even big-ticket items like real estate.
